Help reform copyright!

The European Commission wants to hear your opinion on copyright reform. The aim of this site is to make it easier for you to respond to the official EU consultation by filtering out the questions that do not relate to you directly. Together we can make our opinions and experiences heard!

One more thing: You can answer the question in any of the 24 official European languages you are comfortable with!
